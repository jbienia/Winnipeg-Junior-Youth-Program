Releasing the Potential of Youth


"In the young people of the world...lies a reservoir of capacity to transform society waiting to be tapped"

The Period of Youth

This bright period of youth you share is experience by all - but it is brief, and buffeted by numerous social forces. Although your realities are shaped by a broad diversity of circumstances, yet a desire to bring about constructive change and a capacity for meaningful service, both characterstic of your stage of life, are neither limited to any race or nationality, nor dependent upon material means

The Role of Older Youth

The role that older youth, who relate to the experiences of junior youth aged 11-14, can play in creating an atmosphere that nurtures their spiritual qualities and inspires their zeal for learning and meaningful action is critical. Since junior youth often look up to them how to act, older youth are called to the sacred charge of helping their younger conterparts strengthen their moral foundations
