Friendship, Empowerment, Change, Transformation and Social Action

The Junior Youth Empowerment Program typically engages older youth as animators of younger peers, aged 11-14, to help them develop their powers of expression, capacity for moral reasoning, and an ethic of service to others.

"They are being given the tools needed to combat the forces that would rob them of their true identity as noble beings and to work for the common good."
