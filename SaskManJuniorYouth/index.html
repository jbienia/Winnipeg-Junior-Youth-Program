Junior Youth

Empowerment Program


Friendship, Empowerment, Change, Transformation and Social Action

The Junior Youth Empowerment Program typically engages older youth as animators of younger peers, aged 11-14, to help them develop their powers of expression, capacity for moral reasoning, and an ethic of service to others.

"They are being given the tools needed to combat the forces that would rob them of their true identity as noble beings and to work for the common good."

Releasing the Potential of Youth

The role that older youth, who relate to the experiences of junior youth aged 11-14, can play in creating an atmosphere that nurtures their spiritual qualities and inspires their zeal for learning and meaningful action is critical. Since junior youth often look up to them how to act, older youth are called to the sacred charge of helping their younger conterparts strengthen their moral foundations

Become An Animator

Older youth around the world are recognizing the significant role they can play in the lives of those younger than themselves. They are called animators and serve as trained facilitators for groups.

Junior Youth Groups

The primary function of a group is to serve as an environment of mutual support for a group of friends dedicated to gaining a deeper understanding of the world around them.
